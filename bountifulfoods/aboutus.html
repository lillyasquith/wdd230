About Us

Bountiful Foods was founded by the Miyuki family in 1980. At first, they started to sell their products at the local Public Market Center and had a lot of success. Then, they started to use some national and international suppliers and applied new marketing strategies to expand the company. As a result, the company has been very successful and became very well known.
