Tooele Arts Festival

Tooele Arts Festival is an annual event. This festival takes place over three days toward the end of June in the Tooele City Park. The festival offers free kid's crafts, free entrance, cuisine vendors, amazing stage performers, and various works of art from local vendors and artists including paintings, jewelry, photographs, sculptures and more.

Tooele Valley Railroad Museum and Historical Park

Tooele Valley Railroad Complex was listed on the National Register of Historic Places in 1984. by visiting Tooele Valley Railroad Museum, we can learn more about the regions industries of smelting and mining, and the military history of Tooele County.

Overlake Golf Course

This is a great place for golf lovers. We can play a few rounds and enjoy the wonderful views of mountains, nature, green fields and more.

Benson Grist Mill

Benson Grist Mill was placed on the National Register of Historic Sites in 1972. We can enter the building to see how a gristmill functioned and attend events here including farmers markets and art exhibits.

Great Salt Lake State Park

Great Salt Lake is an important landmark in Utahs scenery. The lake is far saltier than the ocean and a popular spot for migratory birds. We can rent a sailboat or motorboat to go on a water adventure, go camping, and learn about the local ecology in the visitor center.

Tooele County Demographics

There are 72,054 residents in Tooele County, with a median age of 40.5. Of this, 50.76% are males and 49.24% are females. US-born citizens make up 95.45% of the resident pool in Tooele County, while non-US-born citizens account for 2.67%. Additionally, 1.88% of the population is represented by non-citizens. A total of 62,292 people in Tooele County currently live in the same house as they did last year. Source by: Point2homes.
